Hallelujah.

2012 

While walking back from Braigel's one night around this time, Joyce Kooger suddenly stopped me, turned towards me and grabbed me and planted a very, very intense kiss, with lots of tongue. I think it was her attempt to make a move, a gamble, though she was also probably a little drunk and maybe the moment just tickled her. I mentioned my memory of it to her at Karin's wedding and she appeared to be mortified. Yeah, yeah.

And that's that.
